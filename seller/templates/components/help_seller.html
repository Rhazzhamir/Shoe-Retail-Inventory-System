Seller Guide

Welcome to the Seller Dashboard! As a seller, you have the ability to manage your inventory, view orders, and update your products efficiently. Below is a guide to help you navigate through your seller features.

Getting Started

Easy setup, clear instruction. Reach out if needed

Login Instructions

To begin, log in using the credentials provided by the admin. Please note that only the admin can register new seller accounts. Once logged in, you will be redirected to your Seller Dashboard.

Dashboard Overview

The Seller Dashboard serves as your control center. From here, you can view and update your profile, manage product categories, track orders, and more.

Category Management

Manage your product categories by adding, editing, or deleting them. Deleting a category may remove associated products. Proceed with caution.

Product Management

To manage your products, navigate to the Product Management section. To add a new product, click "Add Product" and enter the product details, such as name, price, size, and quantity. To update an existing product, select the product from the inventory list and click "Edit," where you can modify details like price, description, or stock level. If you need to remove a product from your inventory, simply select the product and click "Delete."

Stock Management

Efficient stock management is key to ensuring your inventory is always up-to-date. In the Product Management section, you can easily add new products, update product details (such as price, description, and stock level), or remove discontinued items. Be sure to keep your stock levels accurate to avoid running out of products or overstocking. To update a product, simply select it from the inventory list and click "Edit." To delete a product, select it and click "Delete."

Order Management

Order management is essential for maintaining a smooth shopping experience for customers. In the Order Management section, you can track and manage customer orders from the moment they are placed to delivery. includes an option to export the order data for further analysis or record-keeping. This functionality allows you to export the list of orders and related details directly from the Orders page. The available export formats are Excel.

Notifications

Stay informed with timely notifications about customer orders, stock updates, and product inquiries. In the Notifications section, you can view all the important alerts related to your inventory and orders. Whether it's a low stock alert, an order update, or a customer feedback message, you can stay on top of all actions. Ensure to check this section regularly to take immediate action and keep your business running smoothly.
